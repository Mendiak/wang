Bio

W.A. Wang is an emerging screenwriter from the Netherlands.

She writes deep and compelling stories at the intersection of vulnerability and resilience, exploring uncharted territories and the nuanced landscapes of human existence.

Her first feature screenplay Sh*t in the Blue Locker is an ode to the stigmatized and the misunderstood struggling with mental health conditions.
