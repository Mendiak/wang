Biography

W.A. Wang is an emerging screenwriter from the Netherlands. She writes deep and compelling stories at the intersection of vulnerability and resilience, exploring uncharted territories and the nuanced landscapes of human existence.

Her first feature screenplay 'Sh*t in the Blue Locker' is an ode to the stigmatized and the misunderstood struggling with mental health conditions.

Projects

Screenplays

Sh*t in the Blue Locker

2026

An ode to the stigmatized and the misunderstood struggling with mental health conditions. Two teenagers from contrasting backgrounds forge a quiet alliance in a psychiatric ward, shielding a shared secret from an unyielding system.

Format Feature Length Screenplay (112 mins)
Genre Psychological Drama / Coming-of-Age
Status Completed / Seeking Co-Production

The Silent Coast

2025

An atmospheric study of isolation and memory on the wind-swept shores of Zeeland. An elderly lighthouse keeper is forced to confront the ghosts of his past when an enigmatic traveler washes ashore during a historic storm.

Format Short Film Screenplay (24 mins)
Genre Cinematic Drama / Mystery
Status Produced / Film Festival Run

Echoes of Clay

2024

A blind sculptor struggles with her failing touch while attempting to mold her final and most ambitious exhibition. As her sight fades completely, she embarks on a tactile exploration of her family's unspoken history.

Format Feature Length Screenplay (98 mins)
Genre Arthouse Drama
Status In Development / Supported by Utrecht Script Fund
